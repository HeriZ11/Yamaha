Yamaha's Enduring Story

Yamaha, founded in 1887, began with musical instruments before establishing Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. in 1955. Their first motorcycle, the YA-1, quickly built a reputation for performance and innovation. Driven by the "Kando" philosophy – creating products of exceptional value and excitement – Yamaha continues to be a global leader, synonymous with quality, speed, and adventure in the motorcycle world.
